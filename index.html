Editor's Note

Kanishka Chholak

Founder & Chief Editor

It is with immense pride and heartfelt joy that I welcome you as the Chief Editor of our esteemed magazine, SANGAM. As we unveil the institute magazine, we do so with the hope that the passion, creativity, and dedication we’ve invested will captivate and enrich your experience. For us students, there is no better platform to share our voices and ideas with the world than through the pages of our very own publication. SANGAM has offered us the opportunity to explore the boundless realms of imagination, giving life to our thoughts and perspectives. With each article, photograph, and story, we have sought to capture the most defining moments of the vibrant semester that has just passed, giving you a window into the colourful journey we've all shared. The name SANGAM was not chosen by chance. It symbolizes unity, collaboration, and the convergence of diverse ideas—values that form the very essence of our community. Being a part of this creative endeavor, watching an idea blossom into reality, has been a deeply fulfilling experience for all of us. While we recognize that we still have room to grow, we are committed to improving with each edition, learning from feedback, and refining our vision. Our ultimate goal is to see SANGAM thrive, establishing it as a proud tradition of IIT Jammu. We firmly believe that, with the unwavering support of our peers and the guidance of our esteemed faculty, we can turn this vision into reality. As we continue to navigate through the ever-evolving landscape of student life, I invite you to immerse yourself in the stories and ideas that fill these pages. Thank you for being a part of this journey with us. We value your readership and we look forward to sharing stories that inspire, challenge, and resonate with you in the editions to come. Enjoy the read!
