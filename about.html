About Sangam

Welcome

We are Team Sangam, driven by a singular mission: to bring about meaningful change at IIT Jammu. Our goal is to transform the ordinary and make a lasting impact on the IIT Jammu community. Seeing the need for an official magazine, similar to what we had in school, Kanishka took the initiative to form a team of passionate individuals. Together, we started this journey during our first year, creating something we never thought we could. With the constant support and guidance of Amitash Sir, who has been our go-to person for all challenges, the team has grown significantly in size and enthusiasm. We are excited about our continued growth and the positive changes we aim to bring. Thank you for reading.

Editions

After the end of each semester, we provide every student with a hardcopy of our beloved Sangam. Consisting of all the events that took place in the by gone semester and giving everyone a peek into the memorise they have created and made during the run.

All about IIT Jammu

Sangam is by the student of IIT Jammu and for the students of IIT Jammu. We comprise of all activities and events that officially takes place in the campus. We also decided for the magazine to have a story about Jammu be it the history of Jammu or its traditions etc. We believe the students of IIT Jammu should know the past and present of their dearest Jammu city, and this is just a small step towards it.
